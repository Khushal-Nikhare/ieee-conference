About The Conference

The objective of IC-EETA'25 is to foster a dynamic exchange of knowledge and innovation across the domains of mechanical, civil, electrical and computer engineering. By bringing together leading scholars, industry experts, and visionary practitioners, the conference aims to strengthen academic-industry collaboration and provide a forum for the dissemination of pioneering research.

Emphasizing interdisciplinary synergy, the event is aimed at addressing pressing global challenges while promoting sustainable, cutting-edge practices. Ultimately, it aspires to contribute to the technological growth and sustainable development of our nation.
