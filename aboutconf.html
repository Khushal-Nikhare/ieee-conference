Our Objective

The International Conference on Electrical, Electronics & Technical Applications (IC-EETA'25) is designed to foster a dynamic exchange of knowledge across the domains of mechanical, civil, electrical, and computer engineering.


By bringing together leading scholars, industry experts, and visionary practitioners, the conference aims to strengthen academic-industry collaboration and provide a forum for the dissemination of pioneering research.

Conference Themes

We encourage submissions that explore novel solutions, interdisciplinary approaches, and cutting-edge practices in the following areas:

Sustainable Energy

Renewable energy systems, smart grids, and energy-efficient technologies.

Smart Infrastructure

Civil engineering innovations, smart cities, and sustainable materials.

AI & Robotics

Machine learning applications, automation, and future robotics.

IoT & Cyber Systems

Advanced connectivity, sensors, and cyber-physical system security.

Why Attend?

  • Keynote Speakers: World-renowned experts sharing insights.
  • Networking: Connect with peers from academia and industry.
  • Publications: Opportunity to get your research published in indexed journals.
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions on the latest tools.
